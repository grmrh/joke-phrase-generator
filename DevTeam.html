The inspiration for the joke generator:

Whimsy and humor are serious business! Even the most creative comedy creator will occasionally experience writer's block and everyone needs to practice and refine their craft.

Spontaneity is certainly a key component of humor, but a successful practitioner will engaging in deliberate practice and revision as part of the creative process.

In this spirit, we present the joke generator to assist and guide the creator through the iterative process of exploring and developing ideas.
